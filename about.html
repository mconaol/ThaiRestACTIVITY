Origin Story

We have been serving the freshest and most authentic Thai food to Las Vegas since 1989. As a family owned and operated establishment, we put a high priority on creating a very family friendly atmosphere. Our loyal customer's favorite dishes have been our Thai Beef Jerky, Pad Thai, Tom Yum Soup, Stuffed Chicken Wings and our refreshing Papaya Salad. Stop by and find out what your favorite will be!
